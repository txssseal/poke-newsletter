Sooo without further adooeeee…
PokeVision
Are you ever asking yourself, I wish I could know where all the pokemon are in my city? Maybe you are struggling to find those last two Charmanders to evolve your Charmelon, but you just cannot seem to find those buggers. Well look no further friend you’ve found it! Pokevision.com is the goto site when trying to figure out where pokemon are in your given area. In a nutshell Pokevision has “partnered” with Ninatic(the creators of Pokemon Go), and now has access to show you in a live feed where pokemon are spawning, and how long they will be spawning in a particular location for! Super cool, I know, right?! Anyways this will totes give you the leg up when trying to find those rare Pokes.
Poke Eggs
Remember that feeling when you got your first egg? What about that feeling when you hatched your first egg, super stoked right? What about that feeling when your hatched your egg after you just went on a 5km run, only to find that it was a dang Pidgey!!! This handly little info graphic will give you the low down on what eggs hatch which Pokemon. Use it wisely young grasshoppers.
Poke Evolutions
Having trouble figuring out how much CP power your Poke will gain after you evolve it? Seek no further, the answers are here, and they are free! Pokegotoolkit lets you put in your Pokemons info, and then shows you how strong it will be when you evolve it! Super coooool I know. They say future poke tools are coming soon, so make sure to bookmark this little Gem ☺.
